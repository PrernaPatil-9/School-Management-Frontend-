About Roots to Wings

Welcome to Roots to Wings — a nurturing preschool where every child’s journey begins with love, curiosity, and joy.

Founded in 2008, we believe that the early years are the foundation of a lifetime of learning. Our warm, playful environment helps little ones grow strong roots while giving them the wings to explore, dream, and soar.

With caring teachers, thoughtful activities, and a child-centered approach, we celebrate every small step and big milestone together — because every child deserves to shine.
