Admissions Process

Begin your child's educational journey with Little Wonders Pre-School

Admission Procedure

1

Inquiry & School Visit

Schedule a visit to our campus to see our facilities, meet our teachers, and understand our teaching methodology. You can also request a virtual tour if preferred.

Schedule a Visit arrow_forward
2

Application Submission

Complete the admission application form and submit it along with required documents. Applications are accepted throughout the year based on availability.

3

Child Interaction Session

A friendly, informal interaction session is scheduled where our teachers engage with your child through play-based activities to assess developmental readiness.

4

Admission Confirmation

Upon acceptance, you'll receive an admission offer. Secure your child's seat by completing the fee payment within the specified timeline.

5

Orientation & Start

Attend the parent orientation program and begin your child's exciting learning journey at Little Wonders Pre-School!

Age Criteria

Program Age Requirement Session Starts
Playgroup 2 to 3 years April & September
Nursery 3 to 4 years April & September
LKG 4 to 5 years April
UKG 5 to 6 years April

Important Note

Age is calculated as of March 31st for the academic year starting in April. For mid-year admissions, please contact the admissions office for age eligibility.
